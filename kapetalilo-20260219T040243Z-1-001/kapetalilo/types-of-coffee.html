Types of Coffee

Discover popular coffee drinks from around the world.

Espresso

Strong, concentrated coffee served in a small cup.

Flavor: Bold, intense, smooth crema

Americano

Espresso diluted with hot water for a smooth, mild coffee.

Flavor: Balanced, lighter, subtle crema

Latte

Espresso with steamed milk and a thin layer of foam.

Flavor: Creamy, smooth, mild coffee taste

Cappuccino

Espresso with equal parts steamed milk and foam.

Flavor: Balanced, frothy, slightly stronger than latte

Mocha

Espresso with chocolate and steamed milk, perfect for dessert lovers.

Flavor: Sweet, chocolaty, creamy
